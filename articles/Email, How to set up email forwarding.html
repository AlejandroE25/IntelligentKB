Email, How to set up email forwarding

Edit page
This page contains information about email redirection for your Illinois email.

Important Disclaimers Regarding Email Redirection

  • We encourage all University affiliates to use their provided campus mailbox to ensure all University email is properly delivered. By law, your @illinois.edu email is is subject to audit. This means that if you use your personal email account for @illinois.edu business, that account is also subject to audit. Similarly, if you use your University account for personal purposes, those emails are subject to audit as well.

  • We cannot control what happens to email when you forward or redirect it to another email provider. We've seen cases where email sent to @illinois.edu and redirected to another domain, e.g. @yahoo.com, has been tagged as spam by the receiving email domain. We are not responsible for missing mail to your personal email after it has been forwarded.

    We recommend changing your email address at websites and social media networks to a personal non-forwarded email account as verification emails sent to your @illinois.edu address may not be forwarded.

  • Current faculty and staff are not able to use EDE to set up forwarding for their email. Please see Privacy & Cybersecurity, Faculty and Staff Email Auto-forwarding Retirement FAQ for more information.

How to set up email forwarding

Follow these steps to change where your email messages are delivered.

Help Desk: You are not permitted to change the mail field to a personal address for someone unless it is an email from Banner or their password recovery email. You can change the mail field back to their University email on their behalf. If they wish to set up forwarding to a personal email address that is not on file, you will have to guide them through the process.
  1. Go to the Electronic Directory Editor (EDE) and log in with your NetID and password.
  2. Once you have logged into EDE, find the 'mail' field and enter the address depending on where you want email to go (see options below).
    1. NOTE: Make sure that you type this email address correctly, or you will not receive your email.
      Important: Do not leave this field blank or you will not receive your email! DO NOT ENTER YOURNETID@ILLINOIS.EDU

  3. Click the Apply button at the top of the page to save your changes.

NOTE: Changes will take up to 4 hrs to take effect. Until then, you should check for emails in both the old and new locations.

Option 1: To receive email to your university Exchange account: 

Enter: yournetid@mx.uillinois.edu

(Faculty, staff, graduate and professional students with Exchange accounts, or undergraduates with Office 365 accounts).

Option 2 (Current Students Only): To receive email to your university Google Apps @ Illinois (Gmail) email inbox:

Enter: yournetid@g.illinois.edu

Google Apps @ Illinois is the official title of that mail service, however it is commonly referred to as Illinois Gmail.

Option 3 (Current Students, Alumni, and Former Faculty/Staff): To receive email to your personal email address:

Enter: your personal email address (eg. jdoe@gmail.com)

IMPORTANT: If your university email account is an Exchange inbox, you will also need to follow the steps in this article: [Link for document 47608 is unavailable at this time].

Internal Help Desk Section

As of October 5, 2021, faculty and staff members are not able to make this change on their own. If a faculty or staff member is leaving the university, the Technology Services Help Desk is authorized to change the client's EDE field for email redirection on their behalf.

Students should still be able to edit the mail field by logging into the Electronic Directory Editor.

If anything needs escalated, reassign the ticket to the UIUC-TechServices-Identity and Access Management (Group).
