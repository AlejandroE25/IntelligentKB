Forwarding Email to an External Address

email, forwarding, forward, redirect, NetID, Exchange, Gmail, Outlook
[Internal Staff Note] Always verify the user's NetID is active before assisting with forwarding setup. Escalation path: Help Desk Tier 2 → Exchange Admins.

Overview

University email accounts (Exchange/Outlook) can be configured to automatically forward all incoming messages to an external email address such as Gmail or Yahoo Mail. This is useful if you prefer to read university email in a single inbox.

Important Notes

  • Forwarding is available only to currently enrolled students and active employees.
  • Forwarded messages are still stored in your university inbox (forwarding does not delete them).
  • The university is not responsible for security or privacy of external mail providers.

Steps to Enable Email Forwarding in Outlook on the Web

  1. Sign in to Outlook on the Web at outlook.office.com using your NetID and password.
  2. Click the Settings gear icon in the upper-right corner.
  3. Select View all Outlook settings at the bottom of the panel.
  4. Navigate to Mail → Forwarding.
  5. Check the box Enable forwarding and enter the destination email address.
  6. Optionally check Keep a copy of forwarded messages to retain messages in your university inbox.
  7. Click Save.

Disabling Email Forwarding

  1. Follow steps 1–4 above.
  2. Uncheck the Enable forwarding box.
  3. Click Save.

Troubleshooting

  • If the Forwarding option is greyed out, your account may be restricted. Contact the Help Desk.
  • If forwarded messages are not arriving, check the destination address for typos and verify the external account's spam/junk folder.
  • Changes may take up to 30 minutes to take effect.
